Presentation info

Types of presentations:

Oral

Format: PowerPoint or any other Windows-compatible format, with 4:3 sized slides.

Your presentation has to be emailed to the organizers at csebwpg2016@gmail.com before 12:00pm CDT/1:00pm EDT on Wednesday, June 8. No laptop will be hooked on the day of the presentations. Presenters that do not email their presentation in time will not be able to upload their presentation later on.

Length: 10 minutes, followed by a maximum of 3 minutes for questions for a strict total of 15 minutes. Points will be taken off if time exceeds the 10-min limit and presenters will also have points deducted if no time is left for questions. Presentation will be judged in the language of abstract submission.

Rapid fire

Format for the projection: 1 slide is allowed, without animation or sound. It may be in PowerPoint, PDF or any other Windows-compatible format. It may or may not be a exact replicate of your physical poster; however both the poster and the slide need to represent the same project & data.

Your file has to be emailed to the organizers at csebwpg2016@gmail.com before 12:00pm CDT/1:00pm EDT on Wednesday, June 8. No laptop will be hooked on the day of the presentations. Presenters that do not transfer their presentation in time will not be able to upload their presentation later on. Format of the physical poster: Ideal format is 121cm x 91 cm (4 feet x 3 feet). Maximum space available: 121 cm x 121 cm (4 feet x 4 feet). You will need to bring velcro or tacks to attach your poster on the board.

Length: 3 min. In the manner of a 3 minutes thesis, points will be taken off if time exceeds the 3-min limit. Questions may be asked at the physical poster, but not during the oral part of the presentation. Presentation will be judged in the language of abstract submission.

Poster

Format: Ideal format is 121cm x 91 cm (4 feet x 3 feet). Maximum space available: 121 cm x 121 cm (4 feet x 4 feet). You will need to print your own poster and bring velcro or tacks to attach it on the poster board.

Length: 6-8 minutes to present orally, followed by 2-4 minutes of questions. Points will be taken off if time exceeds the 8-min limit and presenters will also have points deducted if no time is left for questions. Presentation will be judged in the language of abstract submission.

Dinner

Whaka Pimadiziiwii Pinaysiiwigamic (Thunderbird House) is a sacred and grounding place, situated on consecrated grounds; please dress accordingly. Recommended attire: business casual. Please note that you will have to take your shoes off when inside Thunderbird House. You may walk around barefoot, in socks, or in flip-flops.
