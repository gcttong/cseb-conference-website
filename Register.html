Registration details

Registration fees includes workshops, a welcome dinner, a full day symposium and reception, and food and beverages throughout the conference. Registrations are for individual use only and cannot be shared.

To be eligible for the CSEB member rate you must be a member in good standing at the time of the conference. To become a member, please Click Here

Cancellation policy: Written cancellation to csebwpg2016@gmail.com must be received no later than April 29, 2016 to qualify for a refund. A $50 administrative fee will apply. After April 29th there will be no refunds. Substitutions are welcome! In the event you have to cancel and cannot attend, please contact csebwpg2016@gmail.com to confirm the name of the individual who will be replacing you at the conference and adjustments will be made in the registration system.
