Need somewhere to stay?

We have reserved a block of hotel rooms at Canad Inns Destination Centre Health Sciences Centre from June 7th to June 9th. The hotel is connected to the campus (720 William Avenue, Winnipeg MB, R3E 3J7). For toll free reservations call 1-888-33-CANAD (2-2623) and indicate you are booking a room under “U of MB CSEB conference” or “Group #126575”. Canad Inns will provide a guest room rate of $122 per night (plus taxes) based on single or double occupancy in a Standard Guest Room. Canad Inns provides shuttle service between the airport and the hotel. To arrange the shuttle, call in advance to ensure availability.
